SECTION 1: Basic Organic & Reaction Concepts

1. What is nucleophile?
Electron-rich species jo electron donate karta hai.
2. Electrophile kya hota hai?
Electron-deficient species jo electron accept karta hai.
3. SN1 vs SN2 difference?
SN1: 2-step, carbocation
SN2: 1-step, backside attack
4. E1 vs E2?
E1: carbocation
E2: concerted elimination
5. Leaving group kya hota hai?
Jo stable form me molecule se nikal jata hai, e.g., Cl⁻, Br⁻.
6. Carbocation stability order?
3° > 2° > 1° > methyl.
7. Inductive effect kya hai?
Electron withdrawing/donating via sigma bond.
8. Resonance effect?
Electron delocalization via π system.
9. Hyperconjugation kya hai?
Sigma bond interaction with adjacent p-orbital.
10. Aromaticity condition?
Huckel rule: 4n+2 π electrons.
11. Tautomerism kya hai?
Keto-enol interconversion.
12. pKa importance?
Acid strength measure.
13. Polar protic vs aprotic solvent?
Protic: H-bond donor
Aprotic: no H donation
14. Why SN2 faster in aprotic?
Nucleophile strong rehta hai.
15. Steric hindrance kya hota hai?
Bulky groups reaction slow karte hain.
16. Regioselectivity vs stereoselectivity?
Position selectivity vs 3D selectivity.
17. Optical activity kya hai?
Plane polarized light rotate karna.
18. Racemic mixture kya hai?
Equal enantiomers, no net rotation.
19. Meso compound kya hota hai?
Chiral centers but achiral overall.
20. Hammond postulate?
Transition state resembles nearest stable species.

SECTION 2: Reagents & Functional Group Transformations

21. PCC use?
Alcohol → aldehyde.
22. KMnO₄ role?
Strong oxidizing agent.
23. LiAlH₄ vs NaBH₄?
LiAlH₄ stronger.
24. Grignard reagent kya karta hai?
Carbon-carbon bond formation.
25. DIBAL-H use?
Ester → aldehyde.
26. Swern oxidation?
Alcohol → aldehyde/ketone under mild conditions.
27. Hydrogenation catalyst?
Pd/C, Pt.
28. Wittig reaction?
Carbonyl → alkene.
29. Friedel-Crafts reaction?
Alkylation/acylation on aromatic ring.
30. Diazotization?
Aromatic amine → diazonium salt.
31. Sandmeyer reaction?
Diazonium → halide.
32. Reduction of nitro group?
Sn/HCl, Fe/HCl.
33. Oxime formation?
Carbonyl + NH₂OH.
34. Amide formation?
Acid + amine.
35. Ester hydrolysis?
Acid/base catalyzed.
36. Protection group example?
TBDMS for alcohol.
37. Deprotection?
Acidic/fluoride removal.
38. Tosylation?
OH → good leaving group.
39. Alkylation of amine issue?
Over-alkylation.
40. Reductive amination?
Amine synthesis via carbonyl.

SECTION 3: Process Chemistry & Scale-up

41. Process chemistry kya hai?
Lab reaction ko industrial scale pe optimize karna.
42. Scale-up challenge?
Heat transfer, mixing.
43. Exothermic reaction control kaise karte ho?
Cooling, slow addition.
44. Yield vs purity tradeoff?
High yield ≠ high purity always.
45. Impurity kya hai?
Unwanted chemical species.
46. Types of impurities?
Process, degradation, residual solvent.
47. Residual solvent guideline?
ICH Q3C.
48. Class 1 solvents?
Toxic, e.g., benzene.
49. Class 2 solvents?
Limited toxicity.
50. Class 3 solvents?
Low toxic.
51. Recrystallization purpose?
Purification.
52. Polymorphism kya hai?
Same compound, different crystal form.
53. Filtration types?
Vacuum, pressure.
54. Drying methods?
Tray, vacuum.
55. Solvent selection criteria?
Solubility, toxicity.
56. Green chemistry kya hai?
Eco-friendly synthesis.
57. Atom economy?
Useful atoms in product.
58. Reaction mass efficiency?
Yield + waste factor.
59. Work-up kya hota hai?
Reaction ke baad purification.
60. Batch vs continuous?
Batch: traditional
Continuous: modern.

61. TLC purpose?
Reaction monitoring.
62. Rf value kya hai?
Distance ratio.
63. HPLC kya karta hai?
Separation + quantification.
64. GC kab use hota hai?
Volatile compounds.
65. NMR kya batata hai?
Structure.
66. IR spectroscopy?
Functional group detection.
67. Mass spectroscopy?
Molecular weight.
68. UV spectroscopy?
Conjugation.
69. LOD vs LOQ?
Detection vs quantification.
70. Calibration curve kya hai?
Concentration vs response.
71. System suitability kya hai?
Method performance check.
72. Peak tailing kya hai?
Poor separation.
73. Resolution kya hai?
Peak separation quality.
74. Chromatogram kya hai?
Detector output graph.
75. Internal standard kya hai?
Quantification aid.
76. Accuracy vs precision?
True vs reproducible.
77. Robustness kya hai?
Method stability.
78. Validation parameters?
Accuracy, precision, linearity.
79. Impurity profiling?
Identification + quantification.
80. Stability testing?
Drug degradation study.
