Coupling Reactions
R&D Core

Modern Bond Construction

Couplings are route-design workhorses in medicinal chemistry: they connect fragments late, diversify SAR quickly, and enable aryl-aryl, aryl-alkenyl, aryl-alkynyl, C-N, and C-O bonds.
